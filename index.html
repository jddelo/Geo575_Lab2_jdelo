Taking A Closer Look into Education Across Pennsylvania

This interactive display illustrates the ratio of postsecondary degrees to those without a high school diploma by county in Pennsylvania in 2016. Data is classified into three age ranges and is split by gender. Postsecondary degrees include Associate's, Bachelor's, Gradute, and Professional degrees.

Select a category from the dropdown menu to view the results.

Data Sources: "Educational Attainment by Age Range and Gender 2009-2016 County Annual Education" acquired from https://data.pa.gov/Schools-that-Teach/Educational-Attainment-by-Age-Range-and-Gender-200/xwn6-8rmw. County polygons are TIGER/Line Shapefile, Pennsylvania, 2010 Census County and Equivalent State-based from U.S. Department of Commerce, U.S. Census Bureau, Geography Division, Geographic Products Branch.
